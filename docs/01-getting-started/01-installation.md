# Smart Plug Installation Guide

This guide explains how to install a Wi-Fi smart plug for indoor use.

## What’s in the box

- 1 smart plug
- 1 quick-start card
- 1 user manual

## Before you begin

- Confirm the outlet is grounded and in good condition.
- Make sure your phone is connected to a 2.4 GHz Wi-Fi network.
- Do not use the device outdoors or in wet locations.

## Installation steps

1. Plug the smart plug into a standard wall outlet.
2. Wait for the status LED to blink, indicating pairing mode.
3. Open the companion app and tap **Add Device**.
4. Select **Smart Plug** from the list of devices.
5. Follow the on-screen instructions to connect the device to Wi-Fi.
6. Assign a name such as `Desk Lamp` or `Coffee Maker`.
7. Test the outlet by plugging in a small appliance and turning it on from the app.

## Safety notes

- Do not exceed the rated load printed on the device label.
- Unplug the device before cleaning.
- If the plug feels hot, disconnect it immediately and contact support.

## Troubleshooting

| Issue | Possible cause | Fix |
| --- | --- | --- |
| LED does not blink | Device is not powered | Reinsert the plug and try another outlet |
| App cannot find device | Wrong Wi-Fi band | Switch to 2.4 GHz Wi-Fi |

## Maintenance

Wipe the exterior with a dry cloth. Keep vents unobstructed and check the device periodically for damage.
